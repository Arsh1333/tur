What Is Turing Cup?

Turing Cup is the annual flagship competitive programming contest organised by SIESGSTarena - the CodeChef SIESGST Campus Chapter. Currently in its third year, it will be held every spring, with the Qualifier round scheduled in February, and the on-site Finale at the SIESGST Campus in March.The Turing Cup celebrates the spirit of competitive programming and encourages the best programmers from GST and beyond to apply their wits at challenging problem statements to arrive at the most intuitive and efficient solution possible. The Turing Cup is named in honour of Alan Turing - the father of the modern computer.
